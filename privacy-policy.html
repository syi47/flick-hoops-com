Street Flick Hoops — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Street Flick Hoops ("the game", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the game handles. By playing the game you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

The game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. It has no user accounts, no login, and no servers of our own.

All game progress (current level, high scores, stars, coins, unlocked items, and settings) is stored locally on your device only, and is removed if you uninstall the game.

Advertising

The game displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain data, such as a device advertising identifier and approximate usage information. This is governed by Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

Where required (for example in the EEA and UK), the game shows a consent form before serving personalised ads, and you can change your choice at any time using the "Privacy options" button on the main menu. On iOS, the game asks for your permission before tracking (App Tracking Transparency); you can decline and the game still works.

Purchases

The game offers an optional one-time "Remove Ads" purchase, processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed via RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children

The game is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Where required by law, ads are requested in a non-personalised manner.

Your choices

  • Reset all local data by uninstalling the game.
  • Reopen the ad-consent form anytime via "Privacy options" on the main menu.
  • Limit ad tracking in your device settings — iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
